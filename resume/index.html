The Revenge of Dark Lord Tricky-Tooth!': My Time With A 4th Grade Math Class

Play Attention! and Dark Lord Tricky-Tooth

I collaborated with the students to create a math game that they designed. We had level, gameplay, and asset/story design sessions, and I crammed as much of the students' work as I could into the final product. It was great fun, introduced the students to programming, and inspired me to change degrees.

MoodSP

MoodSP Homepage

MoodSP is the result of a week's worth of hard labor during Edinburgh University's 2014 Smart Data Hack. I worked alongside 3 second year Informatics students to incorporate Scottish Pariliamentary meeting transcripts into a small web app. MoodSP scrapes and performs sentiment analysis on meeting text to give an at-a-glance look at an SMP's current mood.

  • Used Python and NLTK to do parsing, POS tagging and sentiment analysis
  • Created a large document database using pymongo and mongodb
  • Performed data scraping with BeautifulSoup
